✦ Good to know

Questions, answered.

The things most people ask before they get started.

Do I need to be vegan or plant-based to train with you?+

Not at all. Plenty of my clients aren't plant-based — your training and nutrition are built around your diet, whether that's plant-based, omnivore or anywhere in between. The plant bit is my story, not a requirement.

I've never boxed or trained before — will I keep up?+

Yes. Most people walk in nervous, and every session is scaled to your level. No experience needed — I'll coach you through proper technique from day one, at your pace.

Where do sessions happen?+

In-person training happens around Southside Glasgow, including outdoor group sessions. Not local? Online coaching works anywhere — custom app workouts, nutrition guidance and weekly check-ins.

How much does it cost?+

It depends what we build together — 1:1 sessions, online coaching or a 12-week block. Book the free 15-minute consult and you'll get exact rates with no pressure, no lock-ins and no hidden fees.

What happens after I send an enquiry?+

It comes straight to me — I personally reply to every enquiry, usually within a day or two. We'll set up a free, no-obligation chat about your goals. No spam, no hard sell.
