03 The story

Boxer first. Plant-powered now.

I'm Joe, based in Southside Glasgow. I've been boxing and training since I was 16, and after years of competing I made the switch to a fully plant-based lifestyle. Today I'm a qualified Level 2 & 3 coach, turning a lifelong passion into a career.

My approach is simple: help you reach your goals — physically and mentally — whatever your lifestyle. Plant-based or not, I'll meet you where you are and build training and nutrition around your life.
